## Overview

The campaign has deployed strategies across four categories: **Primal** (targeting A₁₂ directly), **Dual** (targeting B₄ in C⊥), **Structural** (closing construction routes), and **Thermal** (escape from attractor basins). Each strategy is listed with its status and the result it produced or confirmed.

Total strategies deployed: **30+**  
Active fronts: **2** (primal + dual)  
World records produced: **5**
